“ Student Entrepreneurship Community “ , is a student run body working towards the development of startup ecosystem and building a platform throughout the country. Our vision “Making India Future Ready “can only be achieved when every engineering student start believing in dreams, and works to own & run his own venture. Student Entrepreneurship Community is first of its kind angel investor network of students from varied backgrounds across India. It was started to lead a helping hand with successful events in 2015 and 2019. Following the foot prints of our past events, We are launching “ JAZBAA 1.0 “ with a mission to provide future entrepreneurs a platform to learn trending technologies and pitch their ideas in front of established investors.
